About Us

Musk Wall Systems Corp. delivers interior drywall services for commercial and residential projects including Basement Finish.

Founded in 2005, Musk Wall Systems Corp. was established after President Alceu D. Moreira gained an inside look at how companies and tradesmen interacted when delivering their services. Utilizing his skilled expertise having graduated from a renovation program with focus in drywall, Alceu set out to offer services that prioritize project deadlines without compromising on quality.

Services include:

  • Demolition
  • Light weight Steel frame
  • Insulation
  • Drywall
  • Level 4&5 Plaster Finish
  • Interior Painting
  • Fire Stopping
  • Acoustical Ceiling and Wall Panel System

With over 15 years of industry experience, the company has operated in multiple regions including Ottawa, Calgary, Lethbridge, and Saskatoon. Musk Wall Systems Corp consistently strives to provide quality workmanship and facilitate clients needs to complete their projects efficiently and on budget.
